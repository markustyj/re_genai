 Functional and Non-functional

The first part, 'The product shall be easy for customers with novice skills on the Internet to purchase and watch streaming movies', is a functional requirement as it describes a specific behavior or action that the product must perform.

The second part, 'Customers should be able to purchase and watch a pre-determined movie in under 3 minutes', is a non-functional requirement as it specifies a performance constraint that the product must meet.

 Functional, Non-functional (x2), Temporal (Non-functional subcategory)

Explanation:

* 'The RFS system should be able to easily interface with the BDW environment in order to load the data.': Functional (since it describes a specific action the system should perform)
* '7 data files shall be received from the BDW and loaded into the RFS system within 3 hours one day prior to the start of budgeting process every year.': Functional (describes the number and timing of data files to be loaded)
* 'The data will be as of October month-end.': Temporal (non-functional subcategory, specifies the date of the data)
* 'Test files (7) shall be loaded up to 5 times throughout the year from the BDW.': Functional (describes the number and frequency of test files to be loaded)
