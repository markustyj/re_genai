 Functional and Non-functional. The first part, "The product must be able to interface with various database management systems," is a functional requirement because it specifies a specific action or behavior of the software. The second part, "The product shall communicate successfully with the database management system on 100% of all transactions," is a non-functional requirement because it specifies a performance characteristic or constraint that the software must meet.
